Shruthi Gowda

I'm Shruthi Gowda, a PhD candidate in the Data & AI Cluster at the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science, Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e).

My research explores the intersection of neuro-inspired AI and deep learning, with a particular focus on Cognitive biases, Multi-memory systems, and Sparse coding in the brain — aiming to bridge these cognitive principles with the architecture of deep neural networks.

Prior to academia, I spent over 10 years in industry as an AI Research Engineer and Software Engineer, developing advanced Machine Learning, Deep Learning and embedded software solutions across domains such as Autonomous Driving, Quality Inspection, and Healthcare. Combining industry experience with academic curiosity, I’m passionate about developing intelligent systems that are both computationally efficient and grounded in cognitive principles.

Research Interests: Neuro-inspired Artificial Intelligence, Cognitive Bias, Continual Learning, Robustness, Multi-Modality Learning.

Projects

SAFEXPLAIN (EU Horizon Project)

As Tech Lead for the automotive use case, I designed safety patterns and developed novel explainable AI components for critical embedded systems. This project ensures that AI-driven features in autonomous vehicles adhere to ISO 26262 Functional Safety standards. Automotive Case Study.

ENFIELD (EU Horizon Project)

Collaborated with 15 partners to research and implement adaptive AI algorithms featuring lifelong learning, robustness, and fairness. ENFIELD aims to create trustworthy AI for dynamic real-world environments. Project Site.

NI Machine Vision Library

Led development of a machine vision library integrating deep learning inference with traditional computer vision techniques, shipping robust industrial inspection workflows on NI embedded hardware. Vision Development Module.

Scene Understanding & Object Recognition Suite for ADS

Developed in NavInfo’s Advanced Research Lab, this suite processes multi-camera inputs to detect road users, traffic signs, and lane markers in real-time, supporting autonomous driving systems. NavInfo AI Expertise.

Anonymizer for GDPR Compliance

Implemented an on-device perception module that detects and blurs personally identifiable information such as faces and license plates, ensuring GDPR compliance in video streams.

Change Rate Detection for HD Map Updates

Created algorithms to analyze geo-tagged video feeds and detect changes in road infrastructure, enabling automated HD map maintenance for autonomous vehicle navigation. NavInfo AI Expertise.

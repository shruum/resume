Shruthi Gowda

I'm Shruthi Gowda, a PhD candidate in the Data & AI Cluster at TU Eindhoven.

My research focuses on bridging neuro-inspired cognitive principles with deep neural networks, particularly through Cognitive biases, Multi-memory systems, and Sparse coding in the brain.

Previously, I worked over a decade as an AI Research Engineer and Software Engineer.
